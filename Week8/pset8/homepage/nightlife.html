Nightlife

The excitement of São Paulo does not end with nightfall. Quite the opposite. The city's nightlife is among the greatest local attractions. When the doors of the offices close, the world of São Paulo opens up to the best clubs in Brazil. Almost everyday of the week you can find a place to enjoy the night. The music is varied, as are the environments. From samba sessions to underground hip-hop clubs, to nightclubs that turn into the night with electronic music, São Paulo does not disappoint those looking for entertainment beyond museums and restaurants. The night in São Paulo is inexhaustible, as is the city. Here are just a few suggestions to get you started on the circuit of clubs in São Paulo.

1. Rock

Manifesto Bar

Located in the Itaim Bibi district, the house is shaking the rock scene in São Paulo for more than 20 years. Its environments mix the classic characteristics of a pub and a concert hall. There you can listen to rock, pop, heavy metal and other genres of rock on two floors that accommodate intimate and lively environments. The are walls decorated with photos and instruments autographed by bands such as Kiss, Motorhead, Scorpions, Deep Purple, Adrenaline Mob and Foo Fighters. There are two lounges and great menus of snacks, drinks and sandwiches.

Morrison Rock Bar

One of the most traditional Rock Bars in São Paulo, The Morrison Rock Bar is well know for its high quality cover bands that don't fall short in relation to the sound of the original musicians. With two floors, the second one is usually quieter - there you can drink and eat without bothering with pushing and shoving. The Morrison is located in Vila Madalena, a place known for top-tier bars/clubs.

Bar da Avareza

Located on Augusta Street, one of the most underground places in São Paulo, the Avareza has a self-service system as a differential. There, the customer gets his own beer straight from the taps and very cold. On the other hand, food is not the strong point of the house - there are some snacks like popcorn and "salgadinhos", but that's it. On stage there are cover band performances, tributes to artists and even karaoke!

2. Hip-Hop

Morfeus Club

Real underground Rap in a unique experience that no other house will give you. There are two floors: above are the bar and lounge, in the first floor there is a dance floor and a stage for bands. A complete and cozy place, the vibe of the attractions and the audience envelops you all night long. Located near Barra Funda and Santa Cecília, the Mofeus Club was the brainchild of partners KL Jay (Soweto/Racionais Mc's), Vivian Marques (Soweto), Denise Andriole and Heitor Costamilam (Livaria da Esquina), after five years in partnership to create projects in the music scene, they developed a house that brings together culture and entertainment in São Paulo.

Lions Nightclub

The house is the darling of several tribes in the city – despite the slightly steep price. Very eclectic, it creates an environment open to new possibilities. Its retro decor, 3D-effect runway and a beautiful balcony overlooking the Sé Cathedral contribute to its elegance. The thursday nights take over the hip hop scene in São Paulo, with several attractions and an enviable audience, great presentations happen there with Rap for all tastes.

UP Club

Located in the western region of São Paulo, in the Vila Madalena neighborhood, the UP Club is a night house in the city that was conceived to give an "UP" to everything related to nighttime entertainment in the region. To ensure that the ideas become reality, DJ Puff, considered one of the best DJ's in the Black Music today and expert on the subject, looked for the best sound system and lighting, for his fellow DJ's to do your work in the best possible way.

3. Electronic Music

D.Edge

Located in Barra Funda district, D-Edge, is one of the main electronic music houses in São Paulo, with many awards, it is even highlighted among the 5 most sophisticated clubs in the world. With several floors, the house has a terrace overlooking the Memorial da América Latina, many bars and lighting that provides a sensory experience, with colored lights on the ceiling, walls and floor, following the beat of the music. Since its inauguration in 2003, D-Edge has already received more than a thousand national and international artists.

Clash Club

What started with the intention of just finding a stage that would serve as a home for the Circuito party, turned into what we know today as the Clash Club. Located since 2007 in the region of Barra Funda, in São Paulo, the house has a repertoire that gives place not only to electronic genres but also to the most diverse musical styles. Installed in a renovated warehouse from the 1930s, the club shows that its intentions are directly linked to the essence, faithfully rescuing only what gives satisfaction to the ears. With projections integrated with lighting, the place has three environments, several bars and an outdoor area.

Club A

It has 2 stages with varied musical styles, an incredible garden with a bar, with capacity for around 2000 guests, 3 Tropical Gardens environments, with stage 1 playing all kinds of music, stage 2 playing Techno and Trance and the third environment are the bars and the Cyber Café.The Cyber Café is the place where guests can access the Club A house website for free or pay for access to other websites.In addition, its decoration has many mirrors surrounding the environments, with fascinating lighting and full of colors, we also have air conditioning and generator in the gardens for greater comfort.

4. Samba

Vila Seu Justino

Located in the center of the bohemian district, Vila Madalena, Vila Seu Justino brings an innovative concept, with several pleasant, laid-back environments and yet with the soul of a bar. The public can enjoy the good feijoada on Saturdays, with a lively samba circle, as well as the place that becomes a nightclub bar at dusk. Good food and good drinks, the program can also be followed through social media.

Vila do Samba

Located in Casa Verde, Vila organizes a program aimed at exalting root sambas with artists, composers, singers and drum masters from schools singing on its stage. It also has illustrious neighbors: the samba courts Rosas de Ouro, Camisa Verde e Branco, Unidos do Peruche and Império da Casa Verde.

Boteco Todos os Santos

Located at the beginning of Vila Madalena, Boteco Todos os Santos is the perfect bar for those who like to enjoy a few snacks, have a caipirinha and enjoy the musical performances sitting in a chair or even standing dancing with the group. Relaxed and very comfortable, the bar offers music from Tuesday to Sunday with styles such as pagode, samba and sertanejo.
